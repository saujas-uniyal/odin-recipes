Lasagna

To Index

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Baked to perfection, it’s hearty, flavorful, and comforting. Popular worldwide, lasagna combines textures and savory flavors, making it a favorite for family meals and festive occasions.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Salt and Pepper

Cheese Layer

  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook chopped onions and garlic in a pan, add ground beef or sausage, and brown it. Stir in tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and Italian seasoning. Simmer for 20–30 minutes.
  2. Mix ricotta cheese with an egg, some grated Parmesan, salt, pepper, and optional parsley.
  3. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente (unless using oven-ready noodles). Drain and set aside.
  4. Layer the lasagna with meat sauce and cheese and the noodles
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake 15 to 20 more minutes until bubbly and golden. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.
